About Tuberra Steel

Your Trusted Steel Partner

Tuberra Steel Company is a leading manufacturer and exporter of high-quality prefabricated pipe spools and pipe fittings. We offer customized quality products including Butt-Weld Fittings, Long Radius Bends, Large Diameter Welded Fittings, O'lets, CNC Machining Components, and more.

As a prominent manufacturer, exporter and stockist based in Mumbai, India, we serve a wide range including Carbon Steel, Steel Alloy, Nickel Alloy, Stainless Steel 904L Pipes, Tubes, Pipe Fittings, Flanges, Forged Fittings, Bars & Shafts, Fasteners, and Branch Connections across India and worldwide.
